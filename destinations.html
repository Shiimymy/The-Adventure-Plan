Destinations

Almafi Coast

- 4 full days itinerary -

The Amalfi Coast is a stunning stretch of coastline in southern Italy, overlooking the Tyrrhenian Sea and the Gulf of Salerno. You can visit ancient ruins, medieval churches, and Renaissance gardens that testify to the rich heritage of this region. You can also enjoy the leisure activities that the coast offers, such as boating, snorkeling,swimming, and shopping. The Amalfi Coast is a place where you can experience the best of both worlds: beauty and history.

Istanbul

- 1 week itinerary -

Istanbul is a city that bridges two continents, Europe and Asia, and has a rich history and culture that spans millennia. You can visit magnificent monuments that reflect the legacy of the Byzantine and Ottoman empires. You can also enjoy the vibrant nightlife, the delicious cuisine, and the bustling markets that showcase the diversity and energy of this metropolis. Istanbul is a city that offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Basque country

- 1 week itinerary -

The French Basque Country is a region that combines the charm of the Pyrenees mountains and the beauty of the Atlantic coast. You can discover the rich culture and history of the Basque people, who have their own language and traditions. You can also enjoy the gastronomy, the architecture, and the nature of this diverse and scenic area. The French Basque Country is a place where you can experience a different side of France.

Barcelona & Costa Brava

- 1 week itinerary -

Barcelona and Costa Brava are two beautiful destinations in Spain that offer a perfect blend of history and leisure. The Catalonian coast is home to some of the most stunning beaches in the world, as well as breathtaking architecture and art. Whether you’re interested in exploring the works of Antoni Gaudi or Salvador Dali, or simply relaxing on the beach, there’s something for everyone in Barcelona and Costa Brava.

Northern Ireland

- 4 days itinerary -

Northern Ireland has become a serious tourist draw thanks to its rich culture and natural beauty. The country has a fascinating and complex history with many sites of historical interest. Northern Ireland is also home to some of the most beautiful landscapes in the world. The people are friendly and welcoming, and there is always something new to discover.

Sicily

- 1 week itinerary -

Sicily is a beautiful island with a rich history and culture. The island is famous for its stunning beaches, delicious food, and wine. Sicily has a fascinating history that dates back thousands of years. It has been inhabited by many different cultures over the centuries, including the Greeks, Romans, Arabs, Normans, and more.
